Historical Development Timeline
1973–1990s
Foundation & Hydraulics Era
Phase 01 — Origin
Foundation
1973
Incorporated as Hydraulic Pump Manufacturer
Founded by J.K. Malhoutra in Bangalore. Originally named Dynamatic Hydraulics Limited. Focused on manufacturing hydraulic pumps for agricultural and industrial equipment.
Identity
1973–1990
Building the Hydraulics Foundation
Grew into one of India's leading hydraulic gear pump manufacturers. Built precision engineering discipline and zero-defect manufacturing culture.
Rebrand
1992
Renamed to Dynamatic Technologies Limited
Name change from Dynamatic Hydraulics Limited signaled strategic intent to broaden into technology-driven manufacturing beyond hydraulics alone.
1990s–2005
Aerospace Pivot — Entering Defence
Phase 02 — Transformation Begins
Pivot
Late 1990s
Expansion into Aerospace-Grade Components
Leveraged precision engineering from hydraulics to enter aerospace component and aircraft assembly manufacturing.
Defence
Early 2000s
HAL Partnership — IJT & Sukhoi Su-30MKI
Tied up with Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) for fabrication and assembly of subsonic intermediate jet trainers. Started manufacturing body parts for Sukhoi Su-30MKI and Lakshya jets.
Global OEM
Mid 2000s
Airbus Breakthrough — Flap Track Beams
Began supplying flap track beams to Airbus for A320 family and wide-body A330. A pivotal contract establishing Dynamatic as a credible global aerospace supplier.
2006–2012
Global Acquisitions & Tier-1 Status
Phase 03 — International Expansion
Acquisition
2006
Boeing Contracts — CH-47 Chinook & Bell 407
Secured part contracts with Boeing for CH-47 Chinook helicopter and Bell Textron for Bell 407, cementing multi-OEM global supplier status.
UK Entry
2008
Acquired Oldland CNC, UK — $16 Million
Acquired British aerospace parts manufacturer Oldland CNC for US$16 million. Rebranded as Dynamatic-Oldland Aerospace. Gave Dynamatic facilities in Swindon and Bristol, UK.
Europe
2011
Acquired Eisenwerk Erla GmbH, Germany
Acquired German automotive component maker Eisenwerk Erla with a 630-year legacy. Supplies precision castings to Audi, BMW, Bosch, Volkswagen, Daimler.
Tier-1
2010–2012
Sole Global Supplier — Airbus Narrow-Body FTBs
Became sole global supplier of flap track beam assemblies for ALL Airbus single-aisle aircraft. Also won Boeing P-8 Poseidon maritime aircraft contracts (India, US, Australia navies).
2012–2020
Consolidation & Complex Assemblies
Phase 04 — Deepening Capabilities
Scale
2012–2018
Chinook Ramp & Pylon Assemblies
Developed full ecosystem for Chinook ramp and pylon assembly. Expanded into complex, flight-critical multi-part assemblies — moving up the value chain from components to systems.
R&D
2016
CTO Ravi Joins — HAL Veteran
V. Ravichander joins as CTO from HAL Exports Division, bringing deep aerospace manufacturing and lean systems expertise from Boeing 767 and Mitsubishi programs.
VVIP Visit
2016
UK PM Theresa May Visits Bangalore Facility
UK Prime Minister Theresa May visited Dynamatic's Bangalore manufacturing unit during her India state visit — a testament to the bilateral aerospace partnership.
Investment
2016
TGP Investments Acquires Stake
TGP Investments acquired a significant position in Dynamatic Technologies, providing capital backing for expansion plans.
2020–2023
Pandemic Test & Recovery
Phase 05 — Resilience
Stress Test
2020–2021
COVID-19 — Aviation Supply Chain Disruption
Global aviation collapse hit aerospace revenues sharply. Hydraulics and metallurgy segments acted as stabilizers. Management acknowledged cost overruns and operational gaps without deflection.
Defence Win
Sep 2021
Boeing F-15EX Eagle II Contract
Awarded contract for manufacturing aerostructure assemblies for Boeing's F-15EX Eagle II advanced fighter aircraft — a prestigious US Air Force program.
Recovery
2022–2023
Aerospace Revenue CAGR ~19%
Aerospace segment began strong recovery. Revenue growing at ~19% CAGR from FY22 to FY25 as global aviation demand rebounded and order backlog executed.
2024–2025
Life-of-Program Contracts & New Frontiers
Phase 06 — Platform Partnerships
Flagship
Jan 2024
Dassault Falcon 6X — Life-of-Program Contract
Signed long-term life-of-program contract with Dassault Aviation for flight-critical aerostructures on Falcon 6X business jet. Annuity-like revenues for entire aircraft lifecycle (20–30 years).
New OEM
Apr 2025
Deutsche Aircraft D328eco — Rear Fuselage Line
Inaugurated dedicated rear fuselage assembly line in Bangalore for Deutsche Aircraft's D328eco regional turboprop. Strategic Make-in-India initiative with technology transfer.
Airbus A220
2024
Exclusive Door Supplier — Airbus A220
Became Airbus's exclusive supplier for A220 door assemblies, expanding beyond narrow-body flap track beams to full door systems — a significant move up the value chain.
UK-India
Feb 2026
King's Birthday Celebration — A220 Showcase
Showcased A220 assemblies at the UK King's Birthday Celebration, highlighting UK-India manufacturing synergy.
Partnership
Mar 2026
Hutchinson JV — Aerospace India
Hutchinson and Dynamatic Technologies joined forces to support India's rising aerospace manufacturing ecosystem.
2025+
Next Frontier — UAV, Defence & Autonomy
Phase 07 — Future State
New Vertical
2025
Dynauton Systems — UAV & Anti-Drone Division
Launched Dynauton Systems® developing surveillance UAVs, loitering munitions, defence electronics (gimbals, autopilots, radars) and anti-drone solutions with detection radars.
Digital
2025
Digital Transformation — Salesforce & AR
Implementing Salesforce across organization. Deploying augmented reality (AR) solutions for shop floor support. Led by GM Ahilya Malhoutra (second-gen family).
Advisory

Former IAF Chief Joins Board
Air Chief Marshal VR Chaudhari (Retd.), former 27th Chief of Indian Air Force, joins as Independent Director — strong defence credibility signal.
